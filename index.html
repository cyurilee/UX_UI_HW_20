TRIPEASE

Designing a travel app that assists in allowing users to coordinate trips more effectively with other tripmates
UX Case Study

OVERVIEW

Travelers feel frustrated at the amount of time spent creating a purposeful and time-efficient itinerary. The primary goal of the UX Case Study is to understand the key factors that can reduce frustration in travelers that plan trips in groups. The best outcome is to create a mobile app that can allow tripmates to collaborate efficiently on itineraries and activity decisions with little frustration.
Responsibilities

Leading the design thinking process centered around problem statements, user insights, and a user-centered solution.
